About Me

IT? Mass Comm? This is a question that has made me hesitate for a long time. In the end, I chose IT.

It should be the beginning of my primary school, I found that I was very interested in computer operations. I took part in the school’s computer operation competition if I do not remember wrongly, it was basic Microsoft excel, Webpages design and I won the prizes in four consecutive years. This made me more interested and boost my confidence in computer-related areas. When I was in secondary school, I found that I also liked to study how to plan, shoot, and edit videos. I'm very happy that I have the opportunity to join the School's Sports Day: “Green House TV Station” as a vice-chairperson.

I wish that I can choose the course that I felt interested in further studies. So when I choosing a course for pursuing my bachelor's degree, I had a serious entanglement since both courses I felt interested in.

I'm currently a fresh graduate from INTI International University who pursuing a Bachelor of Computer Science (Hons) major in Business Analytics. I'm very happy that I can study the course that I like and felt interested in. I know that now is the IR4.0, there are many things that are developing rapidly. Knowledge in textbooks is important but not really enough. There are still many things that need to be learned and explored by myself outside the classroom.

In addition, I am also very happy that I did not give up another interest in Mass Comm. I joined INTI IU Mass Communication Club in the first semester and I have the opportunity to become a news anchor, organize large-scale events and plan club activities. These experiences are precious. It made me recognize many different people and also taught me how to communicate with others, plan and etc.

I pay great attention to responsibility and focus on the details. I’m not a clever person but I’m the hardworking person, will always try my best to tackle the tasks!
